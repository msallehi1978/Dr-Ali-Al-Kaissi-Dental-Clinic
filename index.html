About Us

A Legacy of
Clinical Excellence

Established in 2007 in the heart of Dubai, Dr. Ali Al Kaissi Dental Clinic stands as a premier medical centre recognized for delivering world-class dental care. For nearly two decades, we have combined clinical excellence with a uniquely personalized patient approach.

Our facility is designed to provide a warm, highly comforting, and anxiety-free environment — making dental visits completely stress-free for families and busy professionals alike.

  • International sterilisation & safety protocols
  • Digital radiography & intraoral scanning
  • DHA-licensed specialist team
  • Major health insurance accepted
