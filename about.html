About Us

A Premier Centre of
Dental Excellence

Established in 2007 in the heart of Dubai, Dr. Ali Al Kaissi Dental Clinic stands as a premier medical centre recognised for delivering world-class dental care. For nearly two decades, our clinic has combined clinical excellence with a uniquely personalised patient approach that sets us apart in Dubai's competitive healthcare landscape.

We strictly adhere to international sterilisation protocols and utilise state-of-the-art diagnostic machinery, including digital radiography and intraoral scanners, ensuring exceptionally precise diagnoses. Our medical facility is intentionally designed to provide a warm, highly comforting, and anxiety-free environment, making dental visits completely stress-free for families and busy professionals alike.

We accept leading health insurance providers to guarantee that premium dental solutions remain fully accessible to the wider community. Our commitment to transparency, integrity, and clinical excellence has earned us the trust of thousands of families across Dubai and the wider UAE.

  • International sterilisation & safety protocols
  • Digital radiography & intraoral scanning
  • DHA-licensed team of dental specialists
  • Major health insurance plans accepted
  • Anxiety-free, family-friendly environment
  • Personalised treatment plans for every patient

Our Brand Story

A Vision to
Transform Dentistry

The foundation of our clinic was inspired by a visionary commitment to redefine the traditional patient experience in dentistry. In 2007, Dr. Ali Al Kaissi recognised an urgent need in Dubai for a specialised centre that seamlessly merged highly advanced surgical capabilities with deeply empathetic patient care.

Witnessing many individuals delay essential treatments due to severe dental anxiety or past negative experiences, the clinic committed to establishing an environment rooted in complete transparency, gentle techniques, and clinical trust. Over the past nineteen years, the clinic has transformed thousands of smiles.

From a small local practice in Deira, we have evolved into a trusted medical landmark where generations of families confidently entrust their oral health — a legacy we are deeply proud of and fiercely committed to preserving.

2007
Clinic Founded

Dr. Ali Al Kaissi established the clinic in Al Muraqqabat, Deira, with a mission of precision and compassionate care.

2012
Implantology Centre

Introduced advanced 3D-guided implantology, becoming one of Deira's leading implant specialists.

2018
Full Specialty Expansion

Expanded to a full multi-specialty team covering orthodontics, periodontics, paediatrics, and oral surgery.

2025
Trusted Community Landmark

Over 10,000 transformed smiles, 4.9 Google rating, and three generations of families choosing us for their oral health.
